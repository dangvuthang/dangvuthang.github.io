Feedback

My teammate Thang has provided me with some useful feedback regarding my project idea. He told me that my idea is good, and I should add features like recommended courses so that students will have an easier time searching for what they wanted to learn. He also pointed out that since the content of each lecture is up to the teacher to decide, I should let them change the content during the middle of the course so that they can cater to the specific need of each group of students. Thus, setting my application apart from other platforms since their system required the content to be decided up ahead and stick to that till the end. I agree with his idea because it is a unique feature that I believe no other platforms have. By implementing this, I will allow the flexibility that both teachers and students need during their time studying together. Chi and Linh also offer some advice when hearing my idea. At first, I intend to let the price of the whole course be decided by the teacher instead of the weekly/ monthly/ yearly I mentioned in the project idea section. However, they mentioned that doing so may discourage students to enroll in the course because they do not know the quality that the course offer. If they did enroll but turned out bored by the lecture, they might waste a lot of money investing in the course. Even worse, they may stop using the application. As a result, I decided that there should be a trial period for students to check whether this course is right for them, and the fee for any courses will be paid weekly/ monthly/ yearly instead of the full price.
