Team Profile

Before week 8, I was able to assemble other members to form a team. Our team consists of 4 members, which are Nhi, Thang, Chi, and me whose name is also Thang. Since my team does not seem to be very creative when it comes to naming things, we just name our team according to the first letter of everyone’s name. Thus, our team’s name is TTNC. While it is not the best name for the team, I believe it has a nice ring to it and easy to remember.

Although our team is mostly made up of introverted individuals, I believe our team composition is very good. For Nhi, she may not be familiar with coding, but she is experienced with designing UI/UX. She can contribute to building the UI for our final project. Thang may not major in IT; however, with his knowledge coming from the Digital Marketing field, he was able to offer many pieces of advice regarding our idea. His advice helps us to identify whether our idea is practical for the end-user and should we go along with it. Thanks to his advice, we were able to quickly brainstorm many great ideas and avoid wasting too much time on impractical ones. Chi is the only extroverted member in our group. I have not had many chances to speak to her since she studied in another tutorial section. Nonetheless, due to her friendly attitude, she creates an approachable image that helps other teammates to open to each other since we are very timid to start a conversation on our own. You could say that she keeps our team connected and thus improve our teamwork. Lastly, there is me. As I already have 2 years of experience coding, I also offer some technical advice when my teammates need it. I believe I will also be the main coder for my whole team. As you can see from all my team members, they have their strengths and weakness, but together we can compensate for each other to make the final project work. Even if they do not know how to code, they can contribute to the project in their way. Thang has suggested many brilliant ideas while Nhi has started to build the general UI so that we can use it for the final project. Chi is also learning about HTML, CSS, JavaScript to support me for the final project. With amazing teammates like this, I believe we can finish the project on time.
