Project idea

The project that I have been wanting to do for a long time is a virtual classroom. It is a web application that represents a classroom. In this application, instead of going to school, students or anyone wishing to learn new skills can use this website to study anything they want anywhere. Likewise, anyone who is a teacher, or is a professional in a particular field can sign up to share their experience and teach other people.

You can argue that we have already had applications like Google Meet, Microsoft Teams for video conferencing, or if you want to study new things, Udemy will be a famous platform. However, these tools have their limitation when it comes to organizing lectures because Google Meet and Microsoft Team is more for business meetings than for studying. While Udemy is primarily online courses that the teachers record before then upload, the only interaction that teachers and students have is through the question section. This application can be thought of as a combination of both platforms mentioning above. Besides, the COVID-19 has proved that we need another form of learning where teachers and students can interact as in a traditional classroom in case something like this may happen in the future.

For the main website, the application must have authentication features such as login, logout, and signup. I may implement login with other platforms such as Google, Facebook, Twitter, Microsoft, to ease the process of building the backend server. Students should be notified about the upcoming classes, assignments, … Thus, the application should send an email to inform everyone about new notifications. Because there are many courses available, the application will have a search input for users to look for a specific course. Not only that, but there will also be features like filters, sort according to specific criteria that the user required. In addition to the course and lecturer’s description, there will also be a feedback, comment section to provide other users with the experience that the course offers. As students also need to view their schedule for the week, month, and year, the application will have a timetable system of its own. For teachers, they can create a new course and set the start date to inform other users about the upcoming class of their own. There will be a period when students have a chance to enroll free of charge to see whether this course or this style of teaching by the teacher is suitable for them. After that, if they wish to continue with their studies, they need to pay an amount of money required by the course per week/month/year. The beauty of this approach is that the teacher should estimate appropriately what the content of their course worth, and for the students, they can pay as they go meaning if they do not wish to continue learning, they can drop out at any time and save their money. Since the content of the lecture is delivered through real-time interaction with teachers, the content of it will up to date. Unlike platforms like Udemy which you will find content that will only be relevant at the time of recording. The most important of this application is its ability to stream data in real-time on the website for any lectures. When the teacher is ready, they will start the lecture, then the email will be sent to every student to inform everyone who following the course (the lecture time is available through the course description, doing this is to ensure that everyone will be able to join automatically without manual login like other applications). The link will take the students directly to the website; however, as a convention, they will be asked to allow the microphone, and a webcam to continue. The video will have other advanced features like virtual background, breakroom, and other configurations that you will see on any video-conferencing application. Of course, the video and all the materials that the teachers will become available for students to download if they wish to. Another great feature I could think of is the ability to monitor each student’s attention, there will be a tool to monitor whether the students are on the tab focusing on studying, or they just keep the application on while browsing the internet doing something else. That is all the features I could think of as of now.

Since this is a web application, I believe no further hardware is required. All you need is a computer capable of running a web browser and an internet connection. However, the software skills required for this application is quite challenging. As this is a web application, HTML, CSS, JavaScript is the most fundamental skills that one needs for the frontend application. To establish a connection with the frontend application and start streaming video to clients, the backend server is also needed. For the clients to receive data and show it to the user, WebTRC API is something I will have to learn to implement this functionality. I may also want to implement the real-time update, meaning whenever the data on the backend is changed or created, I want it to broadcast its change to all clients that are connecting to it. This functionality also required another API, which is called WebSocket. I believe all these skills are not that hard to come by. You may need some knowledge regarding the mentioned API to make it work; however, nowadays, there are already thousands of libraries, packages that are very easy to use without too much effort to learn.

If the project succeeds, students will have more platforms to learn from. They can learn whatever they want to learn, or further develop their skills through the help of the teacher or professional in the field of their choice. As I have stated in the first paragraph, with the help of this application, students can learn anything anywhere. For those who are teachers, or professionals, they have an opportunity to share their skills, experience with everyone around the world, no longer do they have to worry about the distance between them and the students. Thanks to this application, students are exposed to a wide range of education online. Thus, allowing them to choose the best form of teaching style that is suitable for them.
